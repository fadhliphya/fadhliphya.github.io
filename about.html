About Us

Justus Group: Elevating Culinary Experiences Since 2005

Justus Group is a restaurant industry company founded in 2005 headquartered in Bandung, Indonesia. With a strong commitment to quality and innovation, Justus Group, managed under the auspices of PT. Yuditama Mandiri, boasts 5 main brands: Justus Steak House, Asian Grill Express, Justus Burger & Steak, Hawker Ragam Masakan Indonesia, and Tempayan Indonesian Bistro.

Today, Justus Group has developed an extensive network with a total of 21 outlets spread across the cities of Bandung and Jakarta. Each brand under the Justus Group umbrella offers a unique and captivating culinary experience for its visitors.

Brand Philosophy

"Crafting Culinary Connections, Enriching Lives."

At Justus Group, we believe in the power of culinary excellence to create memorable experiences and foster meaningful connections. Guided by a commitment to quality, innovation, and authenticity, we strive to elevate the dining journey for our guests at every touchpoint. With a dedication to showcasing the rich diversity of flavors from around the world, we aim to delight and inspire, ensuring that every visit to a Justus Group establishment is a celebration of culinary craftsmanship and hospitality. We are driven by a passion for creating moments of joy and satisfaction, enriching lives one meal at a time.
